Just Joe

Design is about telling stories and what better place to start then my own? From the woods of rural Massachusetts to the College of William and Mary to the stunning scenery that is Boulder, hear about my adventures as I hone my design skills and process! Or keep scrolling to skip the story and see some of my work.

Working on a variety of projects, from consulting for a technology start-up to producing cover art for a publishing company, has provided myriad challenges and rewards and proved to be an excellent way to expand my experience. The best part was getting to work closely with clients and the passion we brought to the projects.

From a script and the divergent ideas of directors and designers to a unified world in full-scale brillance, theatrical design is the ultimate interactive design arena! An adrenaline-fueled dash to become an expert on everything and juggling a multitude of requirements, all while fighting against the hard deadline of opening night was an ever-exciting design challenge.

I can't imagine ever creating realistic worlds on stage or helping secure venture capital as Head of Design without first learning traditional art. I may not have been a Picasso or a Michaelangelo when I started, but being able to share concepts pictorially may be the most valuable thing I have learned. In fact, without the ability to sketch, my traveling partner would never have recieved the medical treatment she needed in Hungary.
