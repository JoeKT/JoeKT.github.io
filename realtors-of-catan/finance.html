Financing

It can be a hassle even for the most well-qualified buyers in a normal market. Catan is a little different however; it operates on a barter economy where sheep, wood, clay, ore, and wheat have taken the place of the Dollar and Euro. Not to worry, upon entering the market, every potential buyer recieves some of these resources and they are plentiful enough in Catan. Getting the ones the seller wants can be tricky though and Realtors of Catan is here to help you with great exchange rates and loans.
