Process

Research

Surveys, personas, studying existing competition, and talking to clients, users, friends, and experts: I start every design with research because it leads to more compelling stories.

Wireframing

Quick sketching allows for quick Improvements. I use wireframing for efficient synthesis and evolution of research and requirements.

Mock-ups

My digital and easily replicated mock-ups build incrementally from my wireframes toward a nuanced representation of the end result.

Finished Product

With a high-fidelity mock-up in hand, I start work in the final medium and follow the plan to completion.
